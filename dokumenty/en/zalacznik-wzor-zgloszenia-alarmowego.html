§ 5. Partner Statement

The partner declares that the data indicated in the emergency notification correspond to the current stock levels according to his knowledge and internal documentation. The Partner acknowledges that the notification does not mean an automatic obligation for the Supplier to immediately replenish the warehouse, and that the completion of the addition depends on the principles of the FIXON Partner Programmeme, availability of products, regularity of reporting and the previous settlements of the Partner.

§ 6. Objections clause

The Partner may object to this Standard Alert Report within 7 days of its delivery. The absence of reservations within this deadline means the adoption of a document for information and use in the framework of current cooperation in the FIXON Partnership Programme. In the event of a statement of objections, the Parties shall clarify the content of the document, remove the discrepancies or prepare the final agreed version of the document. The final version of the document to which reservations have been made shall be approved by both Parties in the form of personal signatures or electronic signatures accepted by the Parties, unless otherwise agreed by the Parties in document correspondence.

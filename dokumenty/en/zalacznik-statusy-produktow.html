Nature of the document

This document defines the status of FIXON products used in current cooperation with Partner and provides the basis for copying data to documents concerning price list, starting warehouse, storage support, exposure, testing tools and exclusions from the Sales Result.

Reservations

Reservations to this document may be notified within 7 days of its delivery. The absence of reservations within this deadline means the adoption of a document for information and use in the framework of current cooperation in the FIXON Partnership Programme. Where objections are raised, the final agreed version of the document shall be approved by both Parties in the form of personal signatures or electronic signatures accepted by the Parties, unless otherwise agreed by the Parties in document correspondence.

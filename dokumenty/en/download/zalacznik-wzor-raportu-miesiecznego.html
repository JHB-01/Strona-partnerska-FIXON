§ 6. Partner Statement

The Partner declares that the data indicated in the report are in accordance with his current knowledge, sales documents, stock and events reported during the relevant Settlement Period. The Partner notes that the report provides the basis for the settlement, the determination of the Sales Result, the calculation of the discount, the update of the allocated stock, the evaluation of the alert threshold and the verification of the liability for the products transferred to the Partner.

§ 7. Objections clause

The Partner may object to this Monthly Report template within 7 days of its delivery. The absence of reservations within this deadline means the adoption of a document for information and use in the framework of current cooperation in the FIXON Partnership Programme. In the event of a statement of objections, the Parties shall clarify the content of the document, remove the discrepancies or prepare the final agreed version of the document. The final version of the document to which reservations have been made shall be approved by both Parties in the form of personal signatures or electronic signatures accepted by the Parties, unless otherwise agreed by the Parties in document correspondence.

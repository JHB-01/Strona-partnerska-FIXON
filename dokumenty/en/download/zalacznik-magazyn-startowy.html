Appendix: Starter Stock

Starter stock proposal for a pilot or launch phase, without forcing full warehouse investment.

Working legal/commercial draft. This translated version is prepared for reading, download and local counsel review before use in the selected country.

1. Principle

Starter stock is introduced only when justified by the Partner's channel, customer demand and agreed pilot scope. A full warehouse purchase is not a condition of joining the programme.

2. Product Selection

Products are selected according to customer needs discovered during material purchases and supported by the e-catalogue.

3. Review

Starter stock is reviewed after the first confirmed sales period and may be expanded, reduced or replaced.

Rules for calculating delivery costs

The cost of delivery is calculated on the basis of the gross index price of the product: products cheaper than PLN 100 - PLN 20; products over PLN 200 and below PLN 1000 - PLN 40; products over PLN 1000 - PLN 100; products over PLN 3000 - PLN 300. If the product does not meet the indicated thresholds, the cost of delivery shall be PLN 0 unless the Parties agree otherwise in the individual document.

Reservation clause

Reservations to this document may be notified within 7 days of its delivery to the Partner. The absence of reservations within the above deadline means the adoption of a document for information and use in the framework of current cooperation in the FIXON Partnership Programme. In the event of a statement of objections, the final version of the document agreed by the Parties shall be approved by personal signatures or by electronic signatures accepted by the Parties, unless otherwise agreed by the Parties in document correspondence.

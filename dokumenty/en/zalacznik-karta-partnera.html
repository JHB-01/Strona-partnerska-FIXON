§ 7. Nature of the document

The Partner Card is an information and operational document and defines the current data and parameters of the Partner's cooperation under the FIXON Partnership Programme. The Partner Charter shall not constitute a separate agreement or annex to the Partnership Agreement, unless the Parties expressly agree otherwise in the text of the Charter or in a separate document. In the event of disagreement between the Partner Charter and the Partnership Agreement, the provisions of the Partnership Agreement shall prevail. In the event of a discrepancy between the Partner Charter and the FIXON Partner Programmeme, priority shall be given to the Partner Charter only in terms of individual parameters of cooperation between the Partner, unless it violates the Partnership Agreement.

§ 8. Objections and approval

The Partner may object to the content of this Partner Charter within 7 days of delivery. The absence of reservations within this deadline means the adoption of the Partner Charter for information and use in current operational cooperation. In the event of a statement of objections, the Parties shall clarify the content of the document, remove discrepancies or prepare the final agreed version of the Partner Charter. The final version of the Partner Charter, to which reservations have been raised, shall be approved by both Parties in the form of personal signatures or electronic signatures accepted by the Parties, unless the Parties expressly agree otherwise in document correspondence.
